This project focuses on optimizing logistics and supply chain management through the development of a **Genetic Vehicle Routing Problem (VRP) Optimizer**. Using evolutionary algorithms, specifically **genetic algorithms (GA)** implemented with the **DEAP library**, the optimizer aims to minimize costs like total distance traveled, fuel consumption, and vehicle maintenance while addressing real-world constraints such as vehicle capacities and delivery time windows.
The Genetic VRP Optimizer is designed to enhance operational efficiency and sustainability by reducing carbon emissions and improving route planning for logistics operations. With its scalability and adaptability, the project represents a significant advancement in solving complex VRP instances, aligning with industry demands for smarter, faster, and more environmentally friendly solutions.
